**Product Description:**
The Thunder 5000 is a heavy-duty, oil-lubricated air compressor designed for continuous industrial applications. Its robust cast-iron pump and high-efficiency motor deliver a consistent air flow and pressure, making it ideal for powering pneumatic tools, spray guns, and machinery in workshops and manufacturing plants. The unit features a large, ASME-certified tank for extended operation and a built-in pressure regulator for precise control.

**Technical Specifications:**
* **Horsepower:** 5.0 HP
* **Tank Capacity:** 80 Gallons (ASME Certified)
* **Air Delivery:** 18 CFM @ 90 PSI
